About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Systems Analysis and Planning at Northwestern University, supervised by Prof Yu (Marco) Nie. Prior to joining Northwestern, I got my M.S. degree in Transportation in 2019, supervised by Prof. Xiqun Chen and B.Eng. degree in Civil Engineering in 2016 both from Zhejiang University. My research interests range from economical analysis of ride-hail market, to applications of machine/deep/reinforcement learning in transportation system.

Research Interests

My research focues on transportation economics, transportation network modeling and optimization. In particular, I am working on

  1. Economical analysis of ride-hail market

  2. Shared mobility's impact on current transportation system

  3. Machine/deep/reinforcement learning applications in transportation system
