About me

I am a final-year graduate student majoring in Transportation at Zhejiang University (expect to graduate in June, 2019), and I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiqun (Michael) Chen. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Civil Engineering from the same university in July, 2016. During the Fall of 2017, I was a full-time algorithm engineer intern in DiDi Research, and mainly worked on platform demand/supply prediction.

Currently, I am applying for 19fall Ph.D. program in US. If you want to know about me, please e-mail me and ask for my resume.

Research

My research interests are transportation economics, transportation network modeling and optimization. In particular, I am working on

  1. Economical analysis of ride-sourcing market

  2. Shared mobility's impact on current transportation system

  3. Diverse AI applications in transportation system analysis

    1. Machine Learningc for short-term traffic speed prediction

    2. Deep Learning to forecast the passenger demand over an on-demand ride service

    3. Reinforcement Learning to keep passenger-car matching process high efficiency
