About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Systems Analysis and Planning at Northwestern University, supervised by Prof. Yu (Marco) Nie. Prior to joining Northwestern, I got my M.S. degree in Transportation in 2019, supervised by Prof. Xiqun (Michael) Chen, and B.Eng. degree in Civil Engineering in 2016 both from Zhejiang University. My research interests range from economic analysis of the ride-hail market, to applications of machine/deep/reinforcement learning in the transportation system.

Research Interests

My research focuses on transportation economics, transportation network modeling and optimization. In particular, I am working on

  1. Economic analysis of the ride-hail market

  2. Shared mobility's impact on the transportation system

  3. Machine/deep/reinforcement learning applications in the transportation system
